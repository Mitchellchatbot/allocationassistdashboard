Dr. Mohammed Elkassaby – Consultant Vascular Surgeon – Ireland

Specific areas of interests within the specialization

Minimally invasive complex endovascular interventions for Aortic aneurysms, arterial diseases, diabetic foot, varicose veins. Carotid artery surgery and stroke prevention. Complex dialysis access creation and maintenance and vascular trauma. An experienced vascular surgeon and associate professor, with over 15 years of work experience within university hospitals in Ireland and Egypt. Completed training schemes in both countries. Certified with exit exams from three medical training systems (Irish/UK, European and Egyptian). Experienced in complex and advanced endovascular interventions with an accredited fellowship from the Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland. A published author and reviewer in numerous peer-reviewed journals and book publishing organizations, and a lecturer in multiple international conferences in the field of vascular surgery. Specialist Editor for “The Surgeon”, official journal of the Royal College of Surgeons in Edinburgh and Ireland. An examiner for the European Board of Vascular surgery. .
